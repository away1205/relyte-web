<p>One of the most frequently overlooked tools is the Google search engine. <h4 class="wp-block-heading">The Potential of High Search Volumes</h4>

<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Transactional Keywords Mean Ready-to-Buy Customers</h4>



<p>The beauty of these keywords is their transactional nature. People searching for these terms are often ready to make a purchase. This presents a golden opportunity for businesses to attract and convert high-intent customers.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Why Aren&#8217;t More Businesses Capitalizing on This?</h4>



<p>Despite this, many businesses have not fully embraced the digital presence required in today&#8217;s market. This could be due to a lack of awareness about the importance of a strong digital footprint.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Steps to Enhance Digital Presence</h4>

<li><strong>SEO Optimization:</strong> Improve your website&#8217;s ranking on Google by using relevant keywords and high-quality content.</li>
